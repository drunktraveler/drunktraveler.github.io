Zanarkand and Ruins

  • Find the girl wearing a pink shirt and get Potion (x2) from her
  • Meet Auron and get the Longsword
  • Optional: Get Tidus to half HP and kill Auron, press triangle until you learn Daredevil (170 turns) and Loner (60 turns), then kill the sinspawn appearing infinitely from the back to receive Slayer (100 kills)
  • Kill Esum and continue to the Submerged Ruins
  • Get Potion (x2) from a chest on the left
  • Get 200 Gil from a chest to the right
  • Optional: Load your Al-Bhed progress from a different save
  • Take a left on the bridge and grab a Hi-Potion
  • Kill 2 of the Sahagins and wait for Tidus to escape
  • Get some flint from the room next to the save-sphere
  • Grab an Ether in a room to the right
  • X-Potion somewhere
  • Follow the path for Withered Bouquet and a Hi-Potion at the end
  • Steal a few Grenades from Krik and kill it
  • After waking up on the ship, pick up Al Bhed Primer Vol. I next to the crane
  • Get Potion (x3) from the guy in the northwest
  • Optional: After diving and climbing onto the ship again, the same guy will give you another Potion (x3). You can repeat this process until you have 99 Potions (33 trips)
  • Optional: Set Tidus Overdrive Mode to Loner and kill Rikku in a random encounter. Keep blocking until Tidus Overdrive is available and use it 10/30/80 times to learn Slaughter/Energy Rain/Blitz Ace (est. 1h)
  • Meet Auron and get the Longsword
  • Head into the ruins and kill Tros

Besaid

  • Head into an alcove to the southeast to pick up the Moon Crest
  • West of the beach is a chest with Antidote (x2)
  • Talk to all Aurochs and Wakka to get 200 Gil, Potion (x3), Potion (x2) and a Hi-Potion
  • Follow Wakka and dive. There are three chests in the area containing Antidote (x2), a Phoenix Down and a Hi-Potion
  • Inside the village grab a Phoenix Down to the west
  • Along a narrow westbound path are 400 Gil, a Hi-Potion and Potion (x2)
  • Pick up the Al Bhed Primer Vol. II inside the blue tent and save your game
  • Start the Cloister of Trials
  • Don't forget to solve the puzzle with the Destruction Sphere - leading to a secret room with the Rod of Wisdom
  • After obtaining Valefor, Wakka will give you the Brotherhood
  • Buy what you need from the item shop and talk to the girl with the dog until she tells you her dog found something. Go into the third eastern house and obtain Valefors' Overdrive Energy Blast
  • Get the map after leaving town
  • Beat Kimahri and receive a Hunter's Sword
  • Optional: Return to Besaid and buy equipment
  • Talk to everyone at the beach to obtain 400 Gil, a Remedy, an Ether, a Seeker's Ring and Phoenix Down (x3)

Kilika

  • Board the S. S. Liki and get the Remedy in the cabin
  • Kick the suitcase to obtain potions until you have a total of 20 (nothing happens if you have over 20)
  • Pick up the Al Bhed Primer Vol. III in the power room
  • Optional: Donate money to O'aka XXIII. Once you donate 10.001 Gil his prices will be 30% cheaper than other shops
  • Defeat Sin
  • Defeat Echuilles
  • Rest in Kilika and visit the tavern for the Al Bhed Primer Vol. IV
  • Visit the weapons shop
  • Save a girl from a collapsing house and return to the tavern for an Ether
  • Grab Potion (x3) in the last house
  • Learn Seed Cannon from a Ragora for Kimahri
  • Mana Sphere (x2) can be found east of the entrance
  • Follow the narrow path north for a Scout
  • Further north, then west is a Luck Sphere
  • Talk to a guard for a Remedy
  • Return to the entrance and talk to Luzzu for Antidote (x4)
  • Defeat Lord Ochu by approaching him from the west for Phoenix Down (x3)
  • Talk to Luzzu again for an Elixir
  • Talk to a solider nearby for a Hi-Potion
  • Talk to the guard from earlier to receive a NulBlaze Shield
  • Defeat Geneaux
  • Solve Kilika's Cloister of Trials and grab the Red Armlet for Kimahri
  • Obtain Ifrit, return to Kilika and board the S.S. Winno

Luca

  • Aboard the S.S. Winno, grab the Hi-Potion in the chest
  • Another chance to donate money to O'aka XXIII - give him 10.001 gil for 30% cheaper prices
  • Grab the Al Bhed Primer Vol. V on the bridge
  • Talk to Yuna, then multiple times to Wakka and Lulu
  • Save and head to the deck for a chance to learn the Jecht Shot (Click away 11 messages in 10 seconds)
  • Once in Luca, head northeast and complete the Blitzball tutorial
  • Get to the Main Gate area, move west and down some stairs to grab the Al Bhed Primer Vol. VI and Hi-Potion (x2)
  • Visit the shops
  • Head west and grab the Tidal Spear and 600 Gil north of O'aka
  • Grab Phoenix Down (x2) further northwest
  • At a dock with many boxes, check the minimap for a path and follow it for a HP Sphere and a Magic Sphere
  • Head to the theater area for the Al Bhed Primer Vol. VII
  • Head to the square, then to the outskirts for a chest containing 1.000 Gil
  • Return to the square and enter the café, return to the docks afterwards
  • Optional: Buy a Thunder Spear for Kimahri for easier combat and defeat all workers for Hi- and X-Potions
  • Defeat Oblitzerator
  • Win the Blitzball game for a Strength Sphere
  • Defeat all enemies and revisit the shops before moving on to Mi'ihen Highroad

Mi'ihen Highroad

  • Optional: From now you can compete in Blitzball matches at any of the Save Spheres, allowing you to obtain different rare items as well as three overdrives for Wakka as well as his Mercury Sigil. It is recommended to wait until you have to ability to recruit better players
  • Talk to the old man for a bit of history. Further north, after he stops you, head west for an Ice Brand
  • Have Kimahri learn Explosion from a Bomber
  • Have Kimahri learn Flame from a Doppelhorn
  • Talk to three people around you for Antidote (x2), Hi-Potion and Stichlanze
  • In an alcove to the east Belgemine will challenge you to a fight. Obtain a Seeker's Ring for a loss or an Echo Ring for a win
  • A guy near Belgemine will gift you a Hunter's Spear
  • North of the chocobo is a Remedy
  • Don't kick the ball to obtain Soft (x3) and a man in white will gift you a Red Ring
  • Moving into the next area move into all alcoves and talk to every person for a Lv. 1 Key Sphere, Antidote (x4), 2.000 Gil, 600 Gil, Hi-Potion and Eye Drop (x3)
  • Arriving at the travel agency, if you have another save, you can import your Al Bhed Knowledge
  • Talk to a man for a Lv. 1 Key Sphere
  • Leave to receive Al Bhed Primer Vol. VIII and Mega Potion (x2)
  • Beat Chocobo Eater by pushing it off the cliff for Lv. 1 Key Sphere (x2). Push it 3 times for that to happen - it will fall over after taking 1.200 damage and will be pushed back after another 500 damage
  • Winning allows you to ride Chocobos for free. Ride one, head north and examine a yellow feather to obtain a Heat Lance.
  • Move along for the Al Bhed Primer Vol. IX
  • The next part holds Hi-Potion (x2) in a chest
  • Head southeast and examine more yellow feathers for Thunder Blade and Scout
  • Even further along another feather will lead to a Fortune Sphere
  • This is also your last chance to donate money to O'aka XXIII
  • Pick up the Mars Crest at the end of the road. This is also where you end up if you lose to Chocobo Eater
  • Head back to the main road and talk to Dona. Donate to the guy on the left for items: 100 Gil for a Scout, 1.000 Gil for an Ice Lance, 10.000 Gil for a Moon Ring
  • Moving onto Mushroom Rock, speak with a standing guard for a Hi-Potion and a running guard for an Ether
  • Visit O'akas shop in the northwest
  • Grab a Hi-Potion from the person next to the save sphere
  • Heading into the winding valley, open the chest for 1.000 Gil and talk to the guard next to it for Potion (x10)
  • Moving onto Mushroom Rock, speak with a guard for a Hi-Potion
  • Further along is a Remedy
  • Grab the Hi-Potion underneath the northwestern overhang
  • Take the elevator and speak to the man nearby for an X-Potion
  • Take the western path and another elevator for a Serene Armlet
  • Return up and follow the path until another path west appears, follow it for the Al Bhed Primer Vol. X
  • Return to the main path again and speak to the soldier next to another elevator for a Mega-Potion
  • Go south for a quick cutscene, head up the elevator and go south for another cutscene, then return to the entrance of Mushroom Rock and talk to a guard for a Tough Bangle
  • Return and head into the main command center where you can visit O'akas shop
  • A chest in the northeast contains a Mega-Potion
  • Slightly hidden to the southeast is a Serence Bracer
  • Defeat Gui
  • Defeat Gui again
  • Afer regaining control talk to everyone and head west for a Hi-Potion

Djose

  • As you reach Djose talk to Shelinda for free healing
  • The first man along the road will give you a Soft Ring and the praying man a Variable Steel
  • Pick up Phoenix Down (x2) to the west
  • The robed man nearby will offer you a Hi-Potion
  • Further north slightly east is the Al Bhed Primer Vol. XI
  • An alcove in the wall to the west has a path to a chest with a Bright Bangle
  • The brown-clothed man further up will give you an Ether, the solider further ahead a Mega-Potion
  • Head northeast to Djose Temple and visit the shops
  • In the westernmost part is a chest containing 4.000 Gil and behind the temple another one with Ability Sphere (x4)
  • Inside the temple, get an Ether and a Remedy to the left of the stairs
  • Pick up the Mega Phoenix on the other side of the stairs
  • Solve the Destruction Sphere puzzle and pick up a Magic Sphere
  • Obtain Ixion
  • Pick up a Switch Hitter inside the shop
  • Awaken Yuna and return to the fork before where you could've gone northwest instead. Talk to everybody on the way to receive a Halberd, Hi-Potion (x2), Potion (x10)

Moonflow

  • Talk to Shelinda
  • Pick up the Lv. 1 Key Sphere (x3) on the nearby side-path
  • Find the X-Potion northeast of the Rondos
  • In another alcove on the right are another Lv. 1 Key Sphere (x3)
  • Battle Belgimine's Ixion - You get the Summoner's Soul and Dragon Scale (x2) for winning or Smoke Bomb (x6) for losing
  • The Summoner's Soul allows you to teach your Aeons abilities
  • Grab the Magic Def. Sphere north of Belgimine
  • Visit the shops
  • Open a chest at the southern bank for Phoenix Down (x2)
  • A chest next to Lulu contains 5.000 Gil
  • Defeat Extractor
  • Stock up at O'aka
  • Grab the Al Bhed Primer Vol. XII and the Ether on the nearby incline
  • Welcome Rikku to your party
  • Follow the path and open the chest with Antidote (x4)
  • The next area has another chest with a Mega Potion

Guadosalam and Thunder Plains

  • Unlock Weapon Customization
  • East of Seymour's Residence is a Mega Potion
  • Loot Seymour's House for the Al Bhed Primer Vol. XIII, 3.000 Gil and Hi-Potion (x2)
  • Pass by the hooded person blocking the way to the Thunder Plains for an Elixir
  • On the way to the Farplane hug the left side to find Lightning Marble (x8)
  • Progress the story and leave for the Thunder Plains
  • Dodge lightning nolts consecutively to receive X-Potion (x2), Mega-Potion (x2), MP Sphere (x2), Strength Sphere (x3), HP Sphere (x3), Megalixir (x4), Venus Sigil at 5, 10, 20, 50, 100, 150, 200 consecutive dodges
  • Get hit by lighning bolts to receive an Ether (30 hits) and an Elixir (80 hits)
  • Interact with up to three Qactuar Stone for a chance to battle Qactuars
  • At the start of the road, there is a chest to the west containing Phoenix Down (x2)
  • Northwest of the Save Sphere is High-Potion (x2)
  • Behind the first Qactuar statue is a chest with 5.000 Gil
  • In a northeastern alcove is a Water Ball
  • At the agency, answer Rin's question with the first answer for the Al Bhed Primer Vol. XIV
  • After peeking on Yuno and ending up outside, examine the glowing object for a Yellow Shield
  • Moving onto the next area, grab the X-Potion in the southeast
  • Near the lightning rod, grab the Ether and take the 2.000 further northeast as well as the Remedy to the right of the money

Macalania

  • Arriving at Macalania, follow the only available path and pick up the Sleepy Cait Sith and 2.000 Gil on the way
  • Grab the Phoenix Down (x3) in the next area
  • Play the butterfly minigame if you wish but the real rewards will be available later
  • In the northern part of the woods, find a hidden chest behind a tree containing a Remedy
  • As you arrive at the lake, talk to O'aka and tell him his items are too expensive to lower his prices by 25%
  • Pick up the Al Bhed Primer Vol. XV to the southeast
  • Defeat Spherimorph
  • Receive a Jecht Sphere and Auron's Shooting Star Overdrive
  • Arriving at the Travel Agency, pick up the Al Bhed Primer Vol. XVI to the west
  • Talk to Clasko and his Chocobo and shop
  • Defeat Crawler & Negator
  • Follow the path for a Mega Potion
  • As you arrive at the road, speak with the first person for 400 Gil
  • Inside the temple, talk to Tromell for a Shell Targe
  • Open the chests in the area for X-Potion (x2) and 5.000 Gil
  • Enter the room that somebody runs out of for Remedy (x2) and talk to the person that ran out for Hi-Potion (x2)
  • Loot the other rooms for Phoenix Down (x3) and get an Ether and an Elixir from the men in there
  • Defeat Seymour
  • Solve the Cloister of Trials for the Luck Sphere
  • Pick up the Lv. 1 Key Sphere as you escape
  • Defeat Wendigo
  • Open the chest next to Kimahri for a Lv. 2 Key Sphere
  • Open the chest you saw in the cutscene for an Avenger

Bikanel

  • Arriving at the oasis, you can find Al Bhed Primer Vol. I next to the Save Sphere if you missed it earlier
  • In the southwest corner is a chest with Remedy (x4)
  • Continue on until Lulu, Auron and Wakka join you. Pick up Al Bhed Primer Vol. III next to Wakka if you missed it earlier
  • Obtain Al Bhed Potion (x8) from a chest nearby.
  • Find Kimahri in the next area and grab the Al Bhed Primer Vol. V if you missed it before
  • Open the two chests near Rikku for Ether (x2) and Al Bhed Potion (x8)
  • At the fork, head southwest for Hi-Potion (x4) and interact with the signpost for Al Bhed Primer Vol. XIV if you missed it before
  • Moving into the next area, head north and pick up Hi-Potion (x4) near a rock
  • Southwest from there, pick up X-Potion (x2)
  • Northwest, at the fence, snatch Mega Potion (x2)
  • Grab the Al Bhed Potion (x8) near the Save Sphere
  • Head north and grab a Lv. 2 Key Sphere and 10.000 Gil from a broken structure
  • To the far northeast, grab the Al Bhed Primer Vol. XVII
  • To the far southwest, grab the Elixir
  • From the Save Sphere, pick up the Al Bhed Primer Vol. XVIII close to the exit
  • In the area with the quicksand pits, defeat the enemies for Mercury Crest, Mega-Potion (x2), Megalixir (x3), X-Potion (x2) and Teleport Sphere (x2)
  • Arriving at Home, grab the Al Bhed Primer Vol. XiX as well as Hi-Potion (x2) from a dead body
  • Battling your way through the enemies, grab Al Bhed Potion (x4) from behind you in the main corridor
  • Taking the southern stairs, enter the room on the left. Choose one of nine options from one chest (Bomb battle, Elixir, Hi-Potion, Mega-Potion, Soft, Chimera battle, Potion, Remedy, Evil Eye battle)
  • Open the other chest and choose the second option of the third row, the third option in the second row, the second option of the second row to receive a Friend Sphere
  • Grab the Al Bhed Primer Vol. XX next to the chest
  • Go downstairs and grab Al Bhed Potion (x6) and Al Bhed Primer Vol. XXI
  • The door on the right contains a chest, answer its questions with the third, fourth, second, first answers to receive a Skill Sphere
  • The other chest requires math operations - addition, subtraction, multiplication, addition. Given the numbers 3 and 2 the result would be 3+2 "5" 3-2 "1" 3*2 "6" 3+2 "5" -> "5165". Receive a Special Sphere for your troubles
  • Leave the room and enter the door on the left, move on downstairs for a Lv. 2 Key Sphere and a Lv. 4 Key Sphere
  • Don't forget to grab the 10.000 Gil after the cutscene
  • Obtain and explore your airship! Some Al Bhed will give you Al Bhed Potion (x4)
  • Defeat Evrae

Bevelle

  • Struggle your way into Bevelle's Cloister of Trials
  • Ride the stairs to the bottom and pick up Al Bhed Primer Vol. XXII in the next area
  • Solve the trial for a HP Sphere and Knight Lance
  • Obtain Bahamut
  • Go east at the fork then north to pick up Kimahri and a Mega-Potion
  • Head north to find Auron and a Save Sphere
  • Go west, then south, in the open area go east to find Lulu and a White Magic Sphere behind her
  • Find an Elixir in the southwest corner
  • Examine the glyph in the rubble to warp, going north and east will lead you back to the start
  • Move north, go down a hall to the east and pick up a Black Magic Sphere
  • Step on the glyph, go west, north, west. Wait for the glyph to point west, step on it and be rewarded with a Lucid Ring and a Skill Sphere
  • Go east for 10.000 Gil
  • Return and defeat Isaaru
  • As Tidus, buy something from the chest - because it's hard to run a business as a chest
  • Defeat Evrae Altana - 2 Phoenix Downs or X-Potions will do the trick
  • Loot the two chests along the path for an Avenger and a Rematch
  • Shop at O'aka and level up if required
  • Defeat Seymour Natus
  • Talk to everyone and enjoy the emotional cutscenes
  • Go back one area to grab the Lucid Ring
  • Head west for two areas for a Jecht Sphere
  • Continue on to the Calm Lands

Calm Lands

  • Head to the travel agency in the center of the Calm Lands and equip yourself at Rin's shop on the way
  • Battle Belgemine's Shiva to receive the Aeon's Soul, allowing you to improve Aeon's stats. Also receive Power Sphere (x30) for winning or Speed Sphere (x30) for losing.
  • Grab the Lv. 2 Key Sphere behind the agency
  • Complete the Chocobo Riding minigame
  • In the northwesternmost part, grab the Al Bhed Primer Vol. XXIII
  • In the southeasternmost part, grab 10.000 and 5.000 Gil from the chests
  • A Chocobo feather here will allow you to move to Remiem Temple - completing the temple is close to impossible right now
  • You can grab the Al Bhed Primer Vol. XXIV next to the Save Sphere
  • Complete the Chocobo Race minigame for the Cloudy Mirror as well as an Elixir, a Megalixir and Wings to Discovery (x30)
  • Returning to the chests contaning Gil, there is an alcove to the northeast where the Monster Arena is located. Completeing the quests is not worth it right now however
  • Leave the Calm Lands and defeat Defender X

Mt. Gagazet

  • Defeat Biran Ronso & Yenke Ronso
  • Follow a ramp on the right side of the path for 20.000 Gil
  • Behind some rocks in a small path (visible on the minimap) pick up Mega-Potion (x2)
  • At the fork, head northwest for Braska's Sphere - which should also unlock Aurons Banishing Blade Overdrive if you picked up all Spheres so far
  • Interact with the stone object and go west for a Defending Bracer
  • Update your equipment at Wantz's shop
  • A small path to the south will lead to a Lv. 4 Key Sphere and an HP Sphere
  • Defeat Seymour Flux
  • Grab the Saturn Crest behind a pillar
  • Inside the cave, head northwest into the water, hit the gap in the barrier and pick up the Lv. 1 Key Sphere
  • From the Save Sphere, head northeast, then left, then swim and match the characters to their colors: Rikku - green, Wakka - orange, Tidus blue. You will be rewareded with a Fortune Sphere
  • Returning to the Save Sphere, head northeast again then take a right for a Recovery Ring and a Return Sphere
  • This time head northwest from the Save Sphere for a Pep Talk
  • Head northeast again, then left and left again to meet and defeat the Sanctuary Keeper

FAQ

The foundation of this guide was based on Stephen McNabb's Dark Soul 2 Cheat Sheet source code and DeathGodGarra's NPC Side Quests Guide V2 walkthrough guide

I have feedback, how can I contribute?

You can visit the GitHub repository and report Issues or create a fork and submit a Pull Request.

Is this a guide?

Nope, not really. This is a set of checklists and information that you can use while playing FFX to make sure you don't miss an item or boss.

Do I need to follow the playthrough in this exact order?

You can revisit most areas later on but some items are completely missable. This guide highlights items as they become available and that are missable (WIP), so feel free to follow however you please. You can always check things off in a different order.

Can I use this for multiple save files?

Yup, use the profile selector and buttons in the options tab at the top of the page to setup multiple profiles.

How does the checklist status get saved?

The checklist is saved to your browser's local storage. Be careful when clearing your browser's cache as it will also destroy your saved progress.
